Otafest is Calgary's non-profit, all-ages Japanese anime and pop culture festival featuring anime screenings, an exhibitor hall, community panels, cosplay contests, voice actors, concerts, and more.

Otafest's official website is located at https://otafest.com. Otafest also employs its own link shortening service using URLs beginning with https://otafe.st.
This page is intended to serve as a location for official statistics that may be of use for external parties (e.g. Wikipedia) as provided by the Otafest Film & Cultural Festival Planning Committee.
